Where's The Bus?

This app displays the scheduled stops at any Albuquerque City Bus Stop, along with real-time information based on the GPS locations of the busses. This app is not directly affiliated with the City of Albuquerque or ABQ Ride, but is part of the Albuquerque Open Data Initiative.
